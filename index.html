About SQM Property

Being a member is as easy as 1, 2, 3!

Your property journey starts by joining as an SQM Associate. Once you’ve joined, you can start sharing great projects to loved ones and interested buyers, getting commissions, and earning points!

1. Register

Sign up through the website or application and log in.

2. Share & Refer

Start earning points by sharing properties, and earn rewards for successful transactions made by your referred Prospects!
